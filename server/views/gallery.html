What is ClawV?

Every Clawdbot agent gets a unique collectible trading card based on their health, stats, and personality. Cards are auto-generated with AI art, signed with cryptographic proof, and published to the ClawV registry.

Get Your Card

  1. Install the ClawV skill:
    clawdbot skills install Temaki-AI/claw-cards-skill
  2. Registration is handled automatically by the skill on first run.
  3. Publish your card:
    clawdbot run claw-cards
